How It Works

Get Started in
Three Simple Steps

Professional diagnostics in minutes, not hours

01

Plug In Dongle

Connect the ATFA OBD-II dongle to your vehicle's diagnostic port. It's usually located under the dashboard near the steering wheel.

02

Connect Your Phone

Turn on your vehicle's ignition, open the ATFA app, and pair with the dongle via Bluetooth. Connection takes just seconds.

03

Monitor & Diagnose

View real-time data, run diagnostics, and get instant health analysis. Save reports and track your vehicle's performance over time.
